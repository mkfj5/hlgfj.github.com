Truth Tables Quiz #1

In terms of formal logic, is the statement O => P, where O is ‘5 is an odd number’ and P is ‘Paris is the capital of France’, true?
SOLUTION: Yes, it is true

Truth Tables Quiz #2

SOLUTION: Yes, it is true
