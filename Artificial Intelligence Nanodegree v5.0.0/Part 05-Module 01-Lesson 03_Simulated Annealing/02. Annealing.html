02. Annealing

Annealing

Annealing

Simulated Annealing algorithm is inspired by the annealing process in metallurgy. An annealing process reshapes a hard metal or glass by exposing it to a high temperature and gradually cool it down until it maintains the new shape.

Unlike the Hill Climbing algorithm, which can get stuck in the local maxima, Simulated Annealing is guaranteed to find the global maximum. The next video will explain why simulated annealing is a complete algorithm that is able to find the global maximum.
