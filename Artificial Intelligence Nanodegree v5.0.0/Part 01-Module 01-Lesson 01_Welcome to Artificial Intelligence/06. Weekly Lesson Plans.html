06. Weekly Lesson Plans

Weekly Lesson Plans

The classroom content is grouped by topic, but that structure doesn't provide the most clear indication of what content you should cover each week. The AIND program has a set of weekly lesson plans linked within each topic that you can use to plan ahead for your term, and help monitor your progress so that you stay on track to graduate. The links will appear with headings that follow the pattern in the lesson list: Lesson Plan: Week N

Note that you are free to work ahead of the lesson plans, and you are not required to keep up with the suggested pace. Remember, project deadlines are flexible but the term deadline is not. If you fall behind, you can use the context in the lesson plans to prioritize which lessons are most relevant to completing the projects before your term ends.
