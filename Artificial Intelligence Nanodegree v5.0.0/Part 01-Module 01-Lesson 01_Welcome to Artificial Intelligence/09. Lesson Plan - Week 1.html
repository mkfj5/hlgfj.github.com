09. Lesson Plan - Week 1

# Lesson Plan: Week 1

Your focus this week should be on acclimating to the Udacity platform and completing the first project.

Note: Most of the reading assignments in this course are optional. The optional readings from the textbook Artificial Intelligence: a Modern Approach here—often abbreviated throughout the course as "AIMA"—reinforce and expand the topics in the lectures. We will provide copies of any required reading material, however you will need to obtain your own copy of the textbook for the optional readings. Prior versions of the book are available through the AIMA site through the link above, and current versions are available for rent or purchase online, or you can check with your library to see if they have access to a copy.

Classroom

  • Watch the introduction lessons (Welcome to AI, Intro to AI, Setting up Anaconda, Workspaces, & Solving Sudoku with AI)

    • The References & Resources lesson includes links for Python & Git (which may be useful for project 1)

  • Follow the classroom instructions in Setting up Your Environment to install and configure Anaconda with all the packages and libraries required for the projects and classroom exercises.

  • Complete the first project: Solving Sudoku with AI

    • You may refer to the AIMA textbook chapter 6.2 Constraint Propagation, and chapter 3.1-3.4 on Search (you will read these chapters more carefully in later weeks -- no need to read them in-depth now)

Reading

  • (Optional) Skim AIMA Chapter 1 & 2 - these sections introduce some key terms that may be new and that will be used frequently throughout the program

Announcements

  • The course refund deadline is the end of the first week 1 (7 days after the term starts)
  • The first project Solving Sudoku with AI is due at the end of Week 1
