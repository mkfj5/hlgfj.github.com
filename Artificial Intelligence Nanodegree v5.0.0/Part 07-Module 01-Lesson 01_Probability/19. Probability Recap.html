19. Probability Recap

Probability Formulas

Basic Probability Formulas

  • Probability of event A occurring: P(A)
  • Probability of event A not occurring: P(¬A) = 1 - P(A)
  • Probability of sequential events: P(A,A,A) = P(A) x P(A) x P(A)
  • Joint probability of both event A and event B occurring: P(A,B) = P(A and B) = P(A∧B)
  • Conditional probability of event A given event B occurring: P(A|B)
